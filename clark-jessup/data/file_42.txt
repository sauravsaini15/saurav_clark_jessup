Gear notes - things to think about before fall

Not urgent, just writing it down:

Tent situation:
  The Big Agnes is finally at the end. Two repairs on the pole sleeve.
  Seam tape coming off the fly in the corners. Fine for car camping but
  not for anything where weight matters. Been putting this off because
  of cost. Probably fall 2026 or spring 2027. Check REI sale.

Layering gap:
  Mid-layer is worn. The Patagonia R1 fleece is still fine but the
  zip-neck is starting to fray. Not emergency-level but worth noting.
  Would rather replace before winter than during.

Camera bag:
  Current shoulder bag is not protecting the Z6 body well enough on
  hikes. Getting jostled. Need something with actual padding for the
  body when in the pack. Look at Peak Design or F-stop options.

What I do not need:
  Another pair of trail runners right now. Speedgoats have plenty of
  miles left. Do not buy another headlamp. Two is already too many.
  Do not buy anything from the sale section just because it is cheap.

When to actually look:
  After the alimony math clears in March 2029 things will be different.
  Until then, replace only what breaks or becomes a safety issue.
  The tent is the exception - if it leaks on a real trip that is on me.
